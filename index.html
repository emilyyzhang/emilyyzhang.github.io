Hi! I am a rising fifth-year PhD student in the Operations Research Center at MIT, co-advised by Prof. Georgia Perakis and Prof. Retsef Levi.

My research focuses on developing new analytical methods to address food-related challenges. In collaboration with the Massachusetts Department of Transitional Assistance, I am evaluating the impact of vendor additions on the utilization of a food subsidy program. In partnership with the Metropolitan Council, I am working to improve the allocation of food to local pantries. More broadly, I am interested in enhancing the impact of interventions that promote social good. I am also interested in the design and analysis of combinatorial algorithms.

I completed my undergraduate studies at MIT in 2021 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics. I am grateful to be supported by a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Fellowship and the Ida M. Green Fellowship.
